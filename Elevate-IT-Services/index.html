Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

What types of businesses do you serve?

We specialise in small and medium enterprises across Singapore, including retail, logistics, legal, healthcare, and professional services firms with 10 to 500 employees.

Do you offer on-site support?

Yes. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include on-site support. We can reach most locations in Singapore within 2 hours.

How quickly can you set up our IT infrastructure?

A typical SME network deployment takes 2 to 4 weeks depending on complexity. We start with a site survey and deliver a detailed project timeline before any work begins.

Is our data secure with your managed services?

Absolutely. We follow industry best practices including encryption at rest and in transit, regular penetration testing, and compliance with PDPA (Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act).

Can we scale our plan as we grow?

Yes. All plans are designed to scale. You can upgrade or adjust your plan at any time with no penalties.

What happens if we want to cancel?

We operate on month-to-month agreements with no lock-in contracts. You can cancel with 30 days' written notice.
